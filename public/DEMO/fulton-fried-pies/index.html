Our Story

A Slice of Muskogee History

For decades, Fulton Fried Pies & Bakery has been a cornerstone of the Muskogee community. We believe in the power of a simple, honest recipe. Every pie is hand-rolled, filled with premium ingredients, and fried to that perfect golden crunch that only true tradition can provide.

Our dedication to quality means we never cut corners. Whether you're a lifelong local or just passing through Oklahoma, we welcome you to our table for a taste of home.
