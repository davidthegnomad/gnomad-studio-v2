Practice Founder

Dr. Brant Scott McDaniel

"My mission is to empower families in Muskogee to live their healthiest lives through natural, effective care."

Dr. Brant Scott McDaniel is a highly respected chiropractor serving the Muskogee, Oklahoma community. With an impressive career spanning 41 years, he has dedicated his life to providing personalized healthcare solutions that focus on restoring long-term wellness rather than just treating symptoms.

Education & Expertise

Dr. McDaniel is a proud graduate of the prestigious Logan College of Chiropractic. Throughout his four decades of practice, he has refined his techniques to support patients of all ages, from infants to seniors.

He specializes in treating complex conditions, including:

  • Back Pain During Pregnancy
  • Pediatric Chiropractic Care (Children and Teens)
  • Spinal Alignment and Flank Pain
  • Natural Solutions for Chronic Headaches and Sciatica

Our Philosophy

At McDaniel Family Chiropractic, Dr. McDaniel prioritizes building trusting relationships with his patients. He believes that open communication and education are the keys to a successful health journey. Every treatment plan is tailored to the individual, utilizing proven methods such as spinal manipulation, chiropractic therapy, and natural rehabilitation.
